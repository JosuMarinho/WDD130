Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase a portfolio of my work, demonstrating my skills, experience, and achievements in a visually appealing and easily navigable manner, so that potential employers can review my work and determine if I am a good fit for their professional needs.

Audience

A target audience for my personal website would be companies and organizations looking to hire someone with my skills and experience I aim to present my work and experience in a clear and effective manner, so that potential employers can understand my value as a business researcher and analyst and make an informed decision about whether I am the right candidate for their needs.

Visitors can then navigate to the other sections of the website, which offer a wealth of information about Josu del Angel. The About Me section provides an overview of their background, education, skills, and experience. The Portfolio section features a selection of Josu del Angel's best work examples, including images, videos, and detailed descriptions of each project. Employers looking to learn more about mine's professional qualifications can visit the Resume section of the website, which provides a detailed overview of their education, work experience, and other relevant qualifications. Visitors can also find contact information for me on the website's Contact page, which includes a simple contact form, as well as their email address, phone number, and social media links.
